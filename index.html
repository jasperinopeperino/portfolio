Hi there! I'm Jasper, a graphic designer, type geek and goalkeeper at my local football team. Currently in my third year of Graphic Design at the Royal Academy of Arts. I was born in Maastricht, grew up in 's-Hertogenbosch, and have been living in The Hague since 2021. I like to think these different places have shaped my approach to design. I'm always observing, picking up on overlooked details, and weaving them into my work. For me, design is more than just making something visually pleasing; it's about shifting perspectives and offering people a new way of looking at the world around them.

If you'd like to chat about why we never see a pigeon sitting or why I'm part of Tweeb, please get in touch!
